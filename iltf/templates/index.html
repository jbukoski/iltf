Indian Land Tenure Foundation Webmapping Program

The Indian Land Tenure Foundation Webmapping Program is a joint project between the Indian Land Tenure Foundation (ILTF) and the Spatial Informatics Group - Natural Assets Laboratory (SIG-NAL). The project seeks to facilitate the use of web-based geospatial technologies and data for enhanced land stewardship and informed decision making.
